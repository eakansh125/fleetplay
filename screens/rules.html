Rules

Time limit: 20 minutes

There are many tournaments that take place during the course of the Quiz Olympiad. Each event has its own page on this website briefly explaining its format. This section explains general rules for the whole tournament. Any breach of the rules will likely result in the disqualification of the player(s) involved.

1. Language:

The quizzes are all in English, but you may answer in any official language of a country with a team participating in the Nations Team Quiz at the Quiz Olympiad.

2. Challenges:

If at any time you think your answer should be accepted, then you must complete a challenge slip.

3. Cheating:

Anyone suspected by an official of cheating will be disqualified. You must not use any mobile device during a quiz competition.
